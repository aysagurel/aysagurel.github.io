About Me

I’m Aysenur, a Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher interested in how people interact with and make sense of AI systems. My background includes designing and evaluating conversational systems powered by large language models, at the intersection of HCI and AI.

My research focuses on applying HCI methods to the design of human-AI collaboration, particularly in contexts where people engage in creative and critical knowledge work.
