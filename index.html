About Me

I’m Aysenur Gurel, a prospective Ph.D. student. My interests lie at the intersection of human-computer interaction (HCI) and human-centered AI (HAI), with research experience in designing and evaluating LLM-powered conversational systems.

My research focuses on applying HCI methods to the design of human-AI collaboration, particularly in contexts where people engage in creative and critical knowledge work.
